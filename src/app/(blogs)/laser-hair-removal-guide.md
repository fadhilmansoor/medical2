# Laser Hair Removal: What to Expect

Laser hair removal is a popular treatment for reducing unwanted hair growth with long-lasting results.

## Who is it for?

* People with unwanted hair on face/body
* Anyone looking for smoother skin
* Those who want to reduce razor bumps

## Treatment steps

1. Consultation and skin assessment  
2. Gentle cleansing of the area  
3. Laser pulses (mild heat feeling)  
4. Aftercare instructions

> “Consistency is key. Most clients need multiple sessions for best results.”
— ClinicMaster Dermatology Team

## Aftercare tips

- Avoid sun exposure for 48 hours  
- Use sunscreen daily  
- Avoid hot showers for 24 hours  

### Important note
If you have sensitive skin, please inform the clinic before the session.
