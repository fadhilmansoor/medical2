# Botox Aftercare: Do’s and Don’ts

Botox aftercare is simple but important for best results.

## Do’s

* Stay upright for 4 hours
* Use gentle facial expressions (no heavy massage)
* Drink water and rest

## Don’ts

- Don’t rub the treated area  
- Don’t do heavy workout for 24 hours  
- Don’t lie down immediately  

> “Small precautions can protect your final results.”
— Dr. Sarah

### When will I see results?
Most people notice changes within 3–7 days.
